About The Jain International School "The Legacy"

The Jain International School

Founded by JGI, The Jain International School is one of the Best Schools in K.G.F aims for a holistic approach to educate and guide students with Karnataka State Board curriculum and that ensures comprehensive learning experience. Our school is a co-educational, day-boarding school. We set the highest standards for academic achievement, intellectual growth, ethical awareness and behavior, and sportsmanship for our students.

Ensuring Holistic Development

Education is not just about filling the mind with knowledge; it's about nurturing the heart, expanding the horizons, and ensuring holistic development.

Clubs & Activities

We engage our students in various clubs & activities like linguistics, eco-science, fine arts, math-tech, Performing arts, music, dance & drama to uplift & boost their positive mindset.

Supplementary Activities

To imbibe innovative & creative skills in students, activities on adventure learning, community outreach program, events & celebrations, cooking classes, young engineers program are conducted.

Field Trips

To encourage students in active participation and independent thinking when they come across things that stimulate their interest and curiosity.

Summer Camp

Helps students build character, decision making skills, team work and Leadership skills that guides them in career growth in the future.

Essentials of Budding Entrepreneur

It is a unique training program for Grades 6-8 students to improve their business skills and help them turn into successful entrepreneurs.

Sports Training & Facilities

The Jain International School has state of the art sports complex called “KREEDA” with training programs designed and monitored by national/ international coaches who monitor the progress of the trainees.

Founder- Chairman, Jain Group

Dr. Chenraj Roychand

Dr. Chenraj Roychand, a distinguished Indian educationist, entrepreneur, angel investor and philanthropist with over 40 years of experience, is the Chancellor of JAIN (Deemed-to-be University). His vision to generate a culture of educational excellence has led to the development of numerous successful educational institutions pan India. JAIN (Deemed-to-be University) is one of the prominent institutes among them.

Along with creating educational opportunities, Dr. Roychand also initiated angel-investing firms, which provide the platform for youngsters to become entrepreneurs. Dr. Roychand has been instrumental in incubating and mentoring students. He adopts both traditional and modern approaches to inculcate entrepreneurial zeal and transform their business ideas into successful enterprises. The 40 incubation units at JAIN (Deemed-to-be University) have helped to create employment for 3500 people in a period of 9 years.

JGI is committed to providing the best quality education and mentoring students to be versatile human beings by exposing them to various walks of life, best global practices, and innovative initiatives, thus getting them ready to take up the challenges of life. The JGI team, once a handful of people, is today, a large family comprising children, parents, staff, and associates who are learning and growing by leaps and bounds with several milestones covered and feathers in their cap, the dreams & expectations are now far beyond the horizon. Our students are fully prepared for continued success, beyond their schooling phase.
